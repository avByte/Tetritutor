TETRITUTOR

What is Tetritutor?

Tetritutor is a practice tool! With options to freeplay the block stacking game, practice useful mechanics in specifically setup boards, or be directed to the wiki, Tetritutor puts everything you need to learn the game in one place.

Controls

< - Left Move
> - Right Move
^ Rotate Clockwise
v - Soft Drop
Space - Hard Drop
Shift - Hold Block
Q - Rotate Counterclockwise
W - Rotate Clockwise
